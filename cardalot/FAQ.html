FAQ

 

We at cardalot hope that you are enjoying this app and it is helping improve studying.  Below are common questions that may be what you are looking for.

 

If you do not see your question below please reach out to us.

 

 

How do I create a card and a deck?

- You can add a card from anywhere inside the app by pressing the + button. Decks are created either when adding a card and using the # or on the home screen by selecting Enter a New Deck.

 

What does the Tag do?

- The Tag is a powerful way to create your decks (which are your topics, classes, subjects or anything else).  Eventually you will be able to add multiple tags to a card.

 

Can I re-arrange cards and or decks?

- Great question, currently you cannot re-arrange the decks or cards.

 

How do I delete a card and deck?

- Press and hold on the deck to delete a whole deck. You can edit specific cards inside of the deck.

 

How do I see the back of my card in Quiz Mode?

- As simple as a tap on the card.

 

Is cardalot an awesome and powerful study tool?

- It sure is!

 

Is the cardalot team working on more features?

- We absolutely are! As the features get built out we will let all our awesome users know what great things are coming.

 

What is the difference between cardalot free and pro?

- With caradlot free you get unlimited cards, but only 5 decks. The pro version allows for unlimited decks with more features coming soon!
